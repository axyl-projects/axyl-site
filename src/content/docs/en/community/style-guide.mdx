The purpose of this document is to outline guidelines and specifications for partners and users to create AxylOS graphical assets.

### AxylOS Chibi

AxylOS Chibi has three relevant components: the **Golden Helmet**, the **Medusa Shield** and the **OS Logo** spear.

The colors are shades of the following main ones:
* **Gold** for Helmet
* **Gold** for Medusa Shield
* **Gold** for Forearm Guards
* **Gold** for Greaves
* **Blue** for Helmet Crest
* **Blue** for Clothes
* **Light Grey/Blue** for bottom edge of Clothes
* **Blue/Cyan** for AxylOS Eyes
* **Light Brown** for AxylOS Hair
* **OS Logo** for Spear Tip

Do not alter the character provided to you in any way, including the colors and the relationship
between the elements.
